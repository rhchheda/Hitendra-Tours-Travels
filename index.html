Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the fare?

Our quoted fare includes driver allowance and fuel. Toll taxes, parking fees, and state entry taxes (for outstation trips) are extra and payable as per actual receipts.

What payment modes do you accept?

We accept all major payment modes: Cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking.

Do you track flight delays?

Yes! Our driver monitors your flight status and will be there shortly after you land, even if it's delayed. Free of charge.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel your ride for free up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time.

Will I get live updates?

Yes. Receive real-time booking status, driver details, and vehicle location updates directly on WhatsApp.
